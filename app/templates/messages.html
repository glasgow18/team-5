Leukemia

This chatroom is geared towards useful discussion about Leukaemia

Leukaemia, also spelled leukaemia, is a group of cancers that usually begins in the bone marrow and result in high numbers of abnormal white blood cells. These white blood cells are not fully developed and are called blasts or leukaemia cells.
